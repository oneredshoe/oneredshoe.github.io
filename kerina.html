About Me

I was born in Colorado and lived in Germany and Alaska eventually settling in eastern New York. I am married with two sons who’ve grown into wonderful young men. Having owned and operated a thriving Salon for 20 years I felt a need to reinvent myself. Focusing my passion for expression in a new challenging direction I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Fine Jewelry and Metals Design with a minor in Museum Studies, through the School of American Crafts, at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I am currently in the final months for attaining a Masters degree in Fine Art, also from the Rochester Institute of Technology. These combined experiences equipped and encouraged me to realize a long time goal, to immerse myself in the arts where I can create and share my work with others while bringing awareness to gender inequality.
